About the Presentor

Dr. Amir Rouzati is a physician and aesthetic medicine educator with family‑medicine roots. He founded and scaled a 7‑figure medical aesthetics practice and trains injectors in Canada with a focus on safe, ethical, and evidence‑based care.

His teaching blends clinical judgment with practical business systems—covering patient safety, natural outcomes, team workflows, and sustainable growth for medical professionals entering or expanding in aesthetics.
